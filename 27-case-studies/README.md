# Case Studies: Real-World Vibe Coding Success Stories

## Startup Stories

### Case Study 1: MVP in 48 Hours
- **Team**: 2 non-technical founders
- **Tools**: Cursor + GPT-4
- **Result**: Raised $500K seed round
- **Key**: AI for frontend from Figma, backend scaffolding

### Case Study 2: Solo Founder to Series A
- **Journey**: Non-technical → 15-person team in 2 years
- **Tools**: Copilot daily, Claude for reviews
- **Lesson**: Learning basics first, then building

## Enterprise Adoption

### Fortune 500 Pilot (200+ developers)
- **Results after 6 months**:
  - 40% faster development
  - 30% fewer bugs
  - 85% developer satisfaction
- **Success factors**: Guidelines, guardrails, measurement

## Freelancer Transformations

### Junior to Senior Rates
- **Before**: $50/hr, basic sites
- **After**: $150/hr, full-stack apps
- **Timeline**: 3 months upskilling with AI

## Key Takeaways

### Success Patterns
1. Clear goals
2. Human oversight
3. Iterative approach
4. Documentation
5. Community sharing

### Common Pitfalls
1. Over-reliance on AI
2. Skipping security review
3. Quality drift
4. Knowledge silos

---

**Remember**: AI augments human judgment, doesn't replace it.
